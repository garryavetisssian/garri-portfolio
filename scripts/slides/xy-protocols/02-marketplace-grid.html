XY.Market sells fractional ownership of a digital grid. Each Grid NFT is a zone defined by (X, Y) coordinates and a Layer (1, 2 or 3) that controls how much of the grid it covers. Owners earn a percentage of every ticket bought in their zone and a bonus when a jackpot hits inside it.
