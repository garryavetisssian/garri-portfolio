The product team locked the mascot's anatomy and personality in a tight brand brief. Then I built a generation pipeline — prompt libraries, base-image conditioning, hand-picked seeds, and production polish — that turned every new game system into card art in days, not months.

01 / Brief
Anatomy lock
Silhouette, eye, beak, sailor outfit. The fixed parts of the duck identity.
02 / Prompts
Variant library
A prompt template per job + district, with character traits as parameters.
03 / Generate
Batches of seeds
8–16 candidates per card slot, conditioned on the mascot reference image.
04 / Curate
Pick the keepers
Throw out the off-model outputs; rate the rest on silhouette & vibe.
05 / Polish
Production art
Background separation, frame, rarity stripe, copy. Then into Figma.
