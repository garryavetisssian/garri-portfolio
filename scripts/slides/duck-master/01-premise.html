— The premise

A trading game
built around one duck.

Duck Master is a Telegram mini-app that fuses a stock-market trading loop, card collection, PvP attacks, mini-games and weekly leaderboards — all anchored to a single anthropomorphic duck mascot whose 347 on-screen variants drive every progression beat.

Sector
Mobile · Gaming · Telegram Mini-App

Duration

Scale
